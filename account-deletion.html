Account Deletion & Data Removal

Effective Date: 1st Jan 2026

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Destnly app and log in to your account.
  • Go to the Settings tab from the bottom navigation bar.
  • In the Settings tab, tap on Delete Account.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm and complete your account deletion.
  • If you face any issues, email support@destnly.com from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your profile, matches, messages, and all personal data will be permanently deleted from Destnly's systems within 30 days of your deletion request, unless a shorter period is required by law. This period is in accordance with legally accepted standards for data removal.
  • Some data may be retained for a limited time if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or legal compliance).
  • Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately via Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions.
  • After deletion, your data cannot be recovered.

Google Play Store User Rights

  • Destnly complies with Google Play's account deletion and data removal requirements.
  • Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.
  • For any privacy or data-related concerns, contact privacy@destnly.com.
